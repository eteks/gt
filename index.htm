about us

Globalen Solutions was founded in the year 2008 headquartered at Puducherry-India as a Pure-play entrepreneurial venture with negligible capital as a focused web solutions & e-publishing company. Ever since then, the company has grown at over 200% and now has worked with multiple clients across 3 continents.

Globalen Solutions is close to a 50 member strong team with an existing infrastructure to scale up to 100 professionals. The web being a blend of creativity and technology, the company operates as a Digital Marketing, a Mobile Application creation, a Software Development, an Events Company and a e-publishing & e-book conversions Company. The team comprises of Creative Visualizers, Web Designers, Web Developers, Programmers, Database specialists, Animation Experts, Marketing experts and a dedicated sales and marketing team. Additionally a strong team of Account Managers bridge the gap between various functions and interact with our clients for producing desired results.
